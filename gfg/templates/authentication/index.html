Our Route

This website provides you options to act as a customer as well as a delivery agent. When you opt to be a delivery agent, you get to deliver food and earn money. Ladies' hostel blocks are classified into 2 groups A,B,G,H,Jand C,D,E,F. Men's hostel blocks are classified into 3 groups (A,C,E,H,J,K,M), (B,D,G) and (L,P,Q,R,S,T).So there will be different delivery agents assigned to different groups of blocks. On the website, delivery agents will be able to see the placed orders and the current status of the orders. The delivery agents will accept the orders accordingly with respect to their assigned blocks. Once they accept the order request, you can see the info of delivery agent assigned to you. You will be contact and track them and vice-versa. After collecting your food order from the main gate, the agents will be on their way to bring your food to you. . Once the food is delivered, you are expected to pay the supposed amount to our delivery agent which is determined on the distance between your block and the main gate.
